This is the hangman game used for some Korean artists and groups.
There are a total of 6 tries, but if you guess a letter right the try won't be used.
It lists the letters that have been used and current letters/word indicated by _ _.
This hangman program does have the display image too.
Once the tries are used up/you guess the word correctly a play again question will come up.
You will then be allowed to close out of the program or continue playing.
